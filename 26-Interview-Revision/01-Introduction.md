# Introduction

Interview preparation is not about learning new topics at the last moment.

It is about **revising existing knowledge efficiently**.

This chapter consolidates everything you've learned throughout the repository into a structured revision guide.

---

## Why Revision Matters

Even experienced engineers revise before interviews.

Revision helps you:

- Recall concepts quickly
- Improve confidence
- Reduce mistakes
- Recognize patterns faster
- Solve problems more efficiently
