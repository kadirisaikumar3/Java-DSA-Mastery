# Frequently Asked Questions

## How many DSA problems should I solve?

Focus on understanding patterns rather than a fixed number of problems. Consistent practice is more valuable than quantity.

---

## Which programming language should I use?

Choose one language and master it. Java, C++, and Python are all excellent choices for coding interviews.

---

## How important is Core CS?

Very important. Most product-based companies assess Operating Systems, DBMS, Computer Networks, and OOP concepts alongside DSA.

---

## Is Dynamic Programming mandatory?

Yes. While not every interview includes DP, it is a common topic in medium to hard coding rounds.

---

## Should I memorize solutions?

No. Learn the underlying concepts and problem-solving patterns instead of memorizing code.

---

## How should I prepare before an interview?

- Revise DSA concepts
- Practice coding problems
- Review Core CS
- Participate in mock interviews
- Stay calm and communicate clearly

---

## Final Advice

Consistency, curiosity, and continuous learning are the keys to long-term success in software engineering.
