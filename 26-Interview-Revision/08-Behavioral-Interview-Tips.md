# Behavioral Interview Tips

Technical skills help you get an interview, but behavioral skills often determine whether you receive the offer.

Companies evaluate how you communicate, collaborate, solve problems, and learn from experience.

---

## Common Behavioral Questions

- Tell me about yourself.
- Why do you want to join our company?
- Tell me about a challenging project.
- Describe a difficult bug you fixed.
- Tell me about a failure.
- Tell me about a conflict in a team.
- What are your strengths?
- What are your weaknesses?

---

## STAR Method

Answer behavioral questions using the STAR framework.

### Situation

Explain the context.

### Task

Describe your responsibility.

### Action

Explain the steps you took.

### Result

Share the outcome and what you learned.

---

## Interview Tips

✔ Be honest

✔ Keep answers structured

✔ Focus on impact

✔ Mention lessons learned

✔ Stay positive

---

## Communication

- Speak clearly.
- Think before answering.
- Avoid unnecessary details.
- Be confident, not arrogant.

---

Strong communication combined with solid technical skills creates an excellent interview experience.
