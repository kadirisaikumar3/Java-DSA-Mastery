# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Q1. Does Binary Search always require sorting?

Yes, unless the search space itself is monotonic.

---

## Q2. Why is Binary Search O(log n)?

Because the search space is halved after every iteration.

---

## Q3. Can Binary Search be applied to answers?

Yes.

This technique is called **Search on Answer**.

---

## Q4. Why is mid calculated carefully?

To prevent integer overflow.

---

## Q5. Can Binary Search be recursive?

Yes.

Both iterative and recursive implementations are valid.

---

## Q6. Which Binary Search problems are most important?

- Search in Rotated Sorted Array
- Koko Eating Bananas
- Find Peak Element
- Median of Two Sorted Arrays

---

## Q7. Biggest interview advice?

Don't memorize code.

Understand why Binary Search works on the given search space.
