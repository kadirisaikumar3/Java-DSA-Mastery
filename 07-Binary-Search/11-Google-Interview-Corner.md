# 💼 Google Interview Corner

---

# What Google Evaluates

- Pattern Recognition
- Time Complexity
- Space Complexity
- Communication
- Optimization
- Edge Case Handling

---

# Common Follow-up Questions

- Why Binary Search?
- Why O(log n)?
- Why is the array sorted?
- Can this problem be solved without sorting?
- Why does Search on Answer work?

---

# Edge Cases

- Empty Array
- Single Element
- Duplicate Values
- Target Not Found
- Integer Overflow

---

# Communication Tips

While solving:

- Explain the search space.
- Explain how mid is chosen.
- Explain why one half is discarded.
- State Time & Space Complexity.

---

# Google Advice

Don't just move left and right.

Clearly justify why the discarded half cannot contain the answer.
