# 💼 Google Interview Corner

---

# What Google Looks For

Google interviewers evaluate:

- Correctness
- Time Complexity
- Space Complexity
- Communication
- Problem Solving
- Code Quality

---

# Interview Process

Step 1

Understand the problem.

---

Step 2

Discuss brute-force.

---

Step 3

Optimize.

---

Step 4

Analyze complexity.

---

Step 5

Test edge cases.

---

# Common Follow-up Questions

- Can you optimize your solution?
- Can you reduce the space complexity?
- What happens if the array contains duplicates?
- What if the input size is 10⁷?
- Can this be solved without extra memory?

---

# Communication Tips

While coding, explain:

- What you're doing
- Why you're doing it
- Time Complexity
- Space Complexity

---

# Google Advice

Don't jump directly to code.

Spend time discussing the approach first.

Interviewers value your thinking process.
