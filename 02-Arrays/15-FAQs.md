# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Q1. Why are arrays so important?

Arrays form the basis of many advanced data structures and algorithms.

---

## Q2. Why is array access O(1)?

Because the memory address is calculated directly using the index.

---

## Q3. Why is insertion O(n)?

Elements after the insertion point must be shifted.

---

## Q4. Why is deletion O(n)?

Remaining elements must be shifted to fill the gap.

---

## Q5. Can arrays grow dynamically?

No.

In Java, arrays have a fixed size.

Use ArrayList for dynamic resizing.

---

## Q6. When should I use arrays?

When the size is known and fast index-based access is required.

---

## Q7. What's the difference between Array and ArrayList?

| Array | ArrayList |
|--------|-----------|
| Fixed Size | Dynamic Size |
| Faster | More Flexible |
| Primitive Support | Objects Only |

---
