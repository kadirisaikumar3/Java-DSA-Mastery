# 💼 Google Interview Corner

---

# What Google Evaluates

- Graph Modeling
- BFS & DFS
- Shortest Path
- Union-Find
- MST
- Time & Space Complexity

---

# Common Follow-up Questions

- Why BFS instead of DFS?
- Why Dijkstra?
- Why Union-Find?
- Can it be optimized?
- Can recursion be removed?

---

# Edge Cases

- Empty Graph
- Single Node
- Disconnected Graph
- Cycle
- Self Loop

---

# Communication Tips

While solving:

- Draw the graph.
- Explain traversal.
- Explain algorithm choice.
- Mention Time & Space Complexity.

---

# Google Advice

First identify the graph type:

- Directed / Undirected
- Weighted / Unweighted
- Cyclic / Acyclic

Then choose the algorithm accordingly.
