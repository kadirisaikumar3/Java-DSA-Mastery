# ❌ Brute Force Approach

---

# What is Brute Force?

Traverse every possible path or repeatedly search the graph without using graph properties.

---

# Example

Finding connectivity by checking every pair of nodes repeatedly.

---

# Drawbacks

- Repeated Traversals
- High Time Complexity
- Poor Scalability

---

# Complexity

Often

```
O(V²)

or worse
```

---

# Interview Tip

Always look for BFS, DFS, or Union-Find before considering brute-force solutions.
