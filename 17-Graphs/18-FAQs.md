# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Q1. What is the difference between Tree and Graph?

A Tree has no cycles and is always connected.

A Graph may contain cycles and may be disconnected.

---

## Q2. When should BFS be used?

For shortest paths in unweighted graphs and level-by-level traversal.

---

## Q3. When should DFS be used?

For graph traversal, cycle detection, connected components, and backtracking.

---

## Q4. Which graph problems are most important?

- Number of Islands
- Clone Graph
- Course Schedule
- Network Delay Time
- Word Ladder
- Critical Connections

---

## Q5. Why use Union-Find?

To efficiently solve connectivity and cycle detection problems.

---

## Q6. Which MST algorithm should I choose?

- Kruskal → Sparse Graphs
- Prim → Dense Graphs

---

## Q7. Biggest interview advice?

Always classify the graph before coding:

- Directed / Undirected
- Weighted / Unweighted
- Cyclic / Acyclic
