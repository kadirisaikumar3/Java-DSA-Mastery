# 💼 Google Interview Corner

---

# What Google Expects

Google interviewers evaluate:

- Correctness
- Time Complexity
- Space Complexity
- Communication
- Problem Solving
- Code Quality

---

# Common Follow-up Questions

- Why did you choose HashMap?
- Why not HashSet?
- Can you reduce space complexity?
- What happens during collisions?
- What is the worst-case complexity?

---

# Edge Cases

- Empty Array
- Empty String
- Duplicate Keys
- Negative Numbers
- Large Inputs
- Null Values

---

# Communication Tips

While solving:

- Explain the brute-force solution.
- Explain why hashing improves it.
- Discuss trade-offs.
- State Time and Space Complexity.

---

# Google Advice

Don't just say:

```
HashMap
```

Explain:

- Why it works.
- Why it improves performance.
- Why it's the best choice.
