# 💼 Google Interview Corner

---

# What Google Evaluates

- FIFO Understanding
- Queue Pattern Recognition
- BFS Readiness
- Deque Usage
- Time Complexity
- Space Complexity

---

# Common Follow-up Questions

- Why use Queue instead of Stack?
- Why use Deque?
- Why Circular Queue?
- Can this be optimized?
- Why Priority Queue?

---

# Edge Cases

- Empty Queue
- Single Element
- Full Circular Queue
- Duplicate Values
- Large Input Size

---

# Communication Tips

While solving:

- Explain enqueue/dequeue operations.
- Explain why FIFO is required.
- Mention Time & Space Complexity.
- Discuss why Queue is more suitable than Stack.

---

# Google Advice

Don't simply use a Queue.

Explain:

- Why FIFO is necessary.
- Why each element enters and leaves only once.
- Why the algorithm is efficient.

This demonstrates strong algorithmic thinking.
