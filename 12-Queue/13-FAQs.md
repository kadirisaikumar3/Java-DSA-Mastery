# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Q1. What is the difference between Stack and Queue?

| Stack | Queue |
|--------|-------|
| LIFO | FIFO |
| Insert/Delete at Top | Insert at Rear, Remove from Front |

---

## Q2. What is a Circular Queue?

A queue that reuses empty spaces by connecting the last position to the first.

---

## Q3. Why use a Deque?

To efficiently insert and remove elements from both ends.

---

## Q4. When should I use a Priority Queue?

When elements must be processed based on priority rather than arrival order.

---

## Q5. Which Queue implementation is recommended in Java?

`ArrayDeque` is generally preferred for Queue and Deque operations.

---

## Q7. Biggest interview advice?

Identify whether the problem requires FIFO, priority ordering, or both-end operations before choosing the data structure.
