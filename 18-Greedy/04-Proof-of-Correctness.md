# ✔️ Proof of Correctness

---

# Why is Proof Needed?

A Greedy algorithm is correct only if every local optimal choice leads to the global optimal solution.

---

# Two Conditions

## 1. Greedy Choice Property

Choosing the best option now never prevents reaching the optimal solution.

---

## 2. Optimal Substructure

The optimal solution is built from optimal solutions to subproblems.

---

# Example

Activity Selection

Always choose the activity that finishes earliest.

This leaves maximum time for the remaining activities.

---

# Interview Tip

If you cannot prove the Greedy Choice Property,

Greedy may not be the correct approach.
