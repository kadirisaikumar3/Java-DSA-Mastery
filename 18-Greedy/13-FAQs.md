# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Q1. What is the difference between Greedy and Dynamic Programming?

Greedy never revisits decisions.

Dynamic Programming stores and reuses previous computations.

---

## Q2. Why doesn't Greedy always work?

Because some problems require considering future decisions.

---

## Q3. Which Greedy problems are most important?

- Jump Game
- Gas Station
- Candy
- Fractional Knapsack
- Activity Selection
- Huffman Coding

---

## Q4. Is sorting necessary?

Many Greedy problems begin with sorting, but not all.

---

## Q5. Which companies ask Greedy questions?

---

## Q6. Can Greedy be combined with other techniques?

Yes.

It is commonly combined with:

- Sorting
- Priority Queue
- Graphs
- Heaps

---

## Q7. Biggest interview advice?

Prove why your Greedy choice is always safe before implementing it.
