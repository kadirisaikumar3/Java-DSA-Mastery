# 📘 Chapter 18 - Greedy Algorithms

> "A Greedy Algorithm makes the best local choice at every step, hoping to achieve the global optimum."

---

# 📚 Overview

Greedy Algorithms solve optimization problems by making the locally optimal choice at every step without reconsidering previous decisions.

They are widely used in scheduling, optimization, networking, compression, and resource allocation.
