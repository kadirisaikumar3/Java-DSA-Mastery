# 💼 Google Interview Corner

---

# What Google Evaluates

- Greedy Choice Property
- Optimal Substructure
- Pattern Recognition
- Time Complexity
- Space Complexity

---

# Common Follow-up Questions

- Why Greedy instead of DP?
- Why does Greedy work here?
- Can you prove correctness?
- What if sorting is removed?
- Can this be optimized further?

---

# Edge Cases

- Empty Input
- Single Element
- Duplicate Values
- Already Sorted Input
- Impossible Cases

---

# Communication Tips

While solving:

- Explain the Greedy choice.
- Justify why it is safe.
- Mention Time & Space Complexity.
- Compare with Brute Force.

---

# Google Advice

Don't just write code.

Explain:

- Why the local choice is optimal.
- Why previous choices never need revision.
- Why the algorithm is correct.
