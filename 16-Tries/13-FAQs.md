# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Q1. What is the difference between Trie and HashMap?

A HashMap supports fast exact lookups.

A Trie supports efficient prefix searches as well.

---

## Q2. Why is Trie good for autocomplete?

Words with common prefixes share the same path, making prefix lookups very efficient.

---

## Q3. Why does Trie use more memory?

Each node stores references to multiple child nodes.

---

## Q4. Which Trie problems are most important?

- Implement Trie
- Word Search II
- Search Suggestions System
- Replace Words
- Maximum XOR

---

## Q5. Can Trie store numbers?

Yes.

Binary Tries are commonly used for bitwise problems.

---

## Q6. Can duplicate words be inserted?

Yes, depending on the implementation.

---

## Q7. Biggest interview advice?

If the problem repeatedly searches prefixes or words from a dictionary, consider using a Trie.
