# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Q1. Is Two Pointer always O(n)?

Most implementations are O(n), but sorting beforehand makes the total complexity O(n log n).

---

## Q2. Does Two Pointer require sorting?

Not always.

Many problems like Move Zeroes and Linked List Cycle don't require sorting.

---

## Q3. Why is Two Pointer faster?

Because each pointer usually traverses the array only once.

---

## Q4. Can Two Pointer be used on linked lists?

Yes.

Examples:

- Detect Cycle
- Find Middle Node

---

## Q5. Difference between Sliding Window and Two Pointer?

Sliding Window maintains a continuous range.

Two Pointers solve a broader set of traversal problems.

---

## Q6. What are the most important interview problems?

- Two Sum II
- 3Sum
- Move Zeroes
- Valid Palindrome
- Linked List Cycle
- Trapping Rain Water

---

## Q7. What is the biggest interview tip?

Before writing code, determine which pointer movement strategy fits the problem.
