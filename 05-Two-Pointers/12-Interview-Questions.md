# 🎯 Interview Questions

---

# Beginner

### 1. What is the Two Pointer Technique?

A technique that uses two indices to traverse a data structure efficiently, reducing unnecessary comparisons.

---

### 2. When should you use Two Pointers?

- Sorted Arrays
- Palindrome Problems
- Pair Sum Problems
- In-place Array Modifications

---

### 3. What are the different types of Two Pointers?

- Opposite Direction
- Same Direction
- Fast & Slow Pointers

---

### 4. Why is Two Pointer better than nested loops?

It reduces many O(n²) solutions to O(n).

---

### 5. What is Floyd's Cycle Detection Algorithm?

A fast & slow pointer technique used to detect cycles in linked lists.

---

# Intermediate

### 6. Why does Two Pointer require sorted arrays in many problems?

Sorting enables logical pointer movement based on comparisons.

---

### 7. Difference between Same Direction and Opposite Direction pointers?

| Same Direction | Opposite Direction |
|---------------|--------------------|
| Move left → right | Move from both ends |
| Remove Duplicates | Pair Sum |
| Move Zeroes | Palindrome |

---

### 8. Can Two Pointer be used on Strings?

Yes.

Examples:

- Reverse String
- Valid Palindrome

---

### 9. Can Two Pointer work without sorting?

Yes, for some problems like Move Zeroes and Linked List Cycle.

---

### 10. What is the biggest advantage?

Linear Time Complexity.
