# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Q1. Why is Bit Manipulation faster?

Bitwise operations execute directly at the hardware level.

---

## Q2. Why is XOR so useful?

It removes duplicate values and simplifies many optimization problems.

---

## Q3. Which Bit Manipulation problems are most important?

- Single Number
- Missing Number
- Counting Bits
- Power of Two
- Maximum XOR
- Subsets

---

## Q4. Is Bit Manipulation difficult?

Initially yes, but with practice it becomes one of the easiest optimization techniques.

---

## Q5. Can Bit Manipulation reduce memory usage?

Yes.

It efficiently stores multiple boolean states in a single integer.

---

## Q6. Which companies ask Bit Manipulation questions?

Google, Amazon, Microsoft, Meta, Adobe, Atlassian, Uber, and many more.

---

## Q7. Biggest interview advice?

Understand the binary logic behind every trick instead of memorizing formulas.
