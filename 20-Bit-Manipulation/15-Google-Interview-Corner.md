# 💼 Google Interview Corner

---

# What Google Evaluates

- Binary Thinking
- XOR Tricks
- Bit Masking
- Time Optimization
- Space Optimization

---

# Common Follow-up Questions

- Can you do it in O(1) space?
- Can you remove the HashMap?
- Why does XOR work?
- Can this be solved using bit masking?
- Can you optimize further?

---

# Edge Cases

- Zero
- Negative Numbers
- Large Integers
- Duplicate Values
- Empty Array

---

# Communication Tips

While solving:

- Explain the binary representation.
- Justify the chosen bit operation.
- Mention Time & Space Complexity.

---

# Google Advice

Don't memorize tricks.

Understand why each bit operation works and when to apply it.
