# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Q1. What is the difference between Union Find and DFS?

DFS traverses a graph, while DSU efficiently maintains connectivity as edges are added.

---

## Q2. Why is Path Compression important?

It flattens the tree, making future Find operations much faster.

---

## Q3. Which is better: Union by Rank or Union by Size?

Both provide nearly identical performance and are commonly accepted.

---

## Q4. Can DSU detect cycles?

Yes, in undirected graphs, by checking whether two nodes already have the same parent.

---

## Q5. Why is DSU used in Kruskal's Algorithm?

It efficiently checks whether adding an edge creates a cycle.

---

## Q6. Which companies ask Union Find questions?

Google, Amazon, Microsoft, Meta, Adobe, Atlassian, Uber, Bloomberg, and many more.

---

## Q7. Biggest interview advice?

Understand **why** Path Compression and Union by Rank work instead of memorizing the implementation.
