# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Q1. What is the difference between Heap and BST?

Heap maintains the Heap Property.

BST maintains the ordering rule:

```
Left < Root < Right
```

---

## Q2. Why isn't a Heap sorted?

Because the Heap Property only compares parent and child nodes.

---

## Q3. Why use a Heap instead of Sorting?

Heaps efficiently support repeated insertions, deletions, and Top-K operations.

---

## Q4. Which Heap is used by Java's PriorityQueue?

A Min Heap.

---

## Q5. Which Heap problems are most important?

- Kth Largest Element
- Top K Frequent Elements
- Merge K Sorted Lists
- Find Median from Data Stream
- Task Scheduler

---

## Q6. Can duplicate values exist in a Heap?

Yes.

---

## Q7. Biggest interview advice?

Identify whether the problem needs the smallest, largest, or Top K elements before choosing Min Heap or Max Heap.
