# 💼 Google Interview Corner

---

# What Google Evaluates

- Heap Property
- Priority Queue Usage
- Top-K Pattern Recognition
- Time Complexity
- Space Complexity

---

# Common Follow-up Questions

- Why Heap instead of Sorting?
- Why Min Heap vs Max Heap?
- Can Heapify improve performance?
- Why Build Heap is O(n)?
- Can this be solved without a Heap?

---

# Edge Cases

- Empty Input
- Single Element
- Duplicate Values
- Large K
- K = 1
- K = n

---

# Communication Tips

While solving:

- Explain why a Heap is suitable.
- Describe heap operations.
- Explain why only K elements are maintained (if applicable).
- Mention Time & Space Complexity.

---

# Google Advice

Don't simply use a Heap.

Explain:

- Why the chosen heap type is appropriate.
- How heap operations maintain the required order.
- Why the algorithm achieves the expected complexity.

This demonstrates strong algorithmic thinking.
