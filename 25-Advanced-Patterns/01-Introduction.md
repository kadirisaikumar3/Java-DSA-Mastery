# Introduction

Many beginners believe they must memorize hundreds of coding interview problems.

In reality, experienced engineers solve problems by recognizing **patterns**.

Once you identify the pattern, choosing the right algorithm becomes much easier.

---

## Why Patterns Matter

Different-looking problems often have identical underlying solutions.

Example:

- Pair Sum
- Remove Duplicates
- Container With Most Water

All use the **Two Pointers Pattern**.

---

## Benefits

Learning patterns helps you:

- Solve problems faster
- Improve code quality
- Reduce interview stress
- Build intuition
- Handle unseen questions confidently

---

## Popular Interview Patterns

- Two Pointers
- Sliding Window
- Fast & Slow Pointers
- Merge Intervals
- Binary Search
- DFS / BFS
- Dynamic Programming
- Greedy
- Backtracking
- Union Find

---

## Interview Insight

Top companies rarely test memorization.

They evaluate:

- Problem-solving ability
- Pattern recognition
- Algorithm selection
- Communication
- Code quality

---

Mastering these patterns dramatically improves interview performance.
