# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## 1. Why learn patterns instead of memorizing problems?

Patterns allow you to solve many unseen problems using the same underlying approach.

---

## 2. Which pattern appears most frequently?

- Sliding Window
- Two Pointers
- Binary Search
- DFS/BFS

---

## 3. Are patterns enough for interviews?

Patterns are essential, but strong fundamentals in DSA, communication, and coding style are equally important.

---

## 4. Which pattern is hardest?

Dynamic Programming is often considered the most challenging because it requires defining states and transitions correctly.

---

## 5. How do I identify the right pattern?

Look for clues such as:

- Sorted input
- Contiguous subarray
- Tree or graph
- Interval overlap
- Top K requirement
- Multiple combinations

---

## 6. Should I memorize templates?

Understand the logic first, then practice coding templates until they become natural.

---

## 8. How can I improve pattern recognition?

Practice solving diverse problems and review solutions to understand why a particular pattern was chosen.
