# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Q1. Is Backtracking the same as Recursion?

No.

Backtracking uses recursion, but recursion alone is not backtracking.

---

## Q2. Why is Backtracking slow?

Because it may explore an exponential number of possibilities.

---

## Q3. What is Pruning?

Skipping branches that cannot produce a valid answer.

---

## Q4. Why do we undo choices?

To restore the previous state before exploring another possibility.

---

## Q5. Can Backtracking be optimized?

Yes.

Using:

- Pruning
- Memoization (problem dependent)
- Constraint checking

---

## Q6. Which Backtracking problems are most important?

- Subsets
- Permutations
- Combination Sum
- N-Queens
- Sudoku Solver
- Word Search

---

## Q7. Biggest interview advice?

Visualize the decision tree before writing code.
