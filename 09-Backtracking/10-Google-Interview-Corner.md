# 💼 Google Interview Corner

---

# What Google Evaluates

- Recursive Thinking
- Decision Tree Design
- State Space Exploration
- Pruning Strategy
- Time Complexity
- Space Complexity

---

# Common Follow-up Questions

- Why Backtracking?
- Why not Brute Force?
- What is Pruning?
- How do you avoid duplicate solutions?
- Can this solution be optimized?

---

# Edge Cases

- Empty Input
- Duplicate Values
- Invalid Choices
- No Valid Solution
- Large Search Space

---

# Communication Tips

While solving:

- Explain the recursive function.
- Describe the decision tree.
- Explain pruning conditions.
- Justify time and space complexity.

---

# Google Advice

Don't just explain the recursive calls.

Clearly explain:

- Why a choice is made.
- Why it is undone.
- How pruning reduces unnecessary exploration.

This demonstrates strong problem-solving skills.
