# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Q1. Why are Trees recursive?

Because every subtree is itself a Tree.

---

## Q2. Why is DFS preferred for most Tree problems?

It naturally processes recursive structures and subtree information.

---

## Q3. When should BFS be used?

For level-by-level traversal and shortest path in unweighted trees.

---

## Q4. What is the difference between Height and Depth?

Height measures downward distance to a leaf.

Depth measures distance from the root.

---

## Q5. Why is Postorder important?

It computes child results before the parent, making it ideal for Diameter, Balance, and similar problems.

---

## Q6. Which Tree problems are most important?

- Maximum Depth
- Diameter
- Lowest Common Ancestor
- Path Sum
- Right Side View
- Serialize & Deserialize

---

## Q7. Biggest interview advice?

Draw the Tree before writing code and identify whether DFS or BFS is the best approach.
