# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Q1. Is Sliding Window always O(n)?

Yes, in most implementations each element is visited at most twice.

---

## Q2. Does Sliding Window always require arrays?

No.

It also works on:

- Strings
- Lists
- Streams

---

## Q3. Difference between Sliding Window and Two Pointers?

Sliding Window maintains a contiguous window.

Two Pointers is a broader technique that may not always maintain a window.

---

## Q4. When should I use a Fixed Size Window?

When the window length is given.

---

## Q5. When should I use a Variable Size Window?

When the problem asks for the longest or shortest valid window.

---

## Q6. Which problems are most important?

- Longest Substring Without Repeating Characters
- Minimum Window Substring
- Fruit Into Baskets
- Find All Anagrams

---

## Q7. Biggest interview advice?

Understand why the window expands and shrinks instead of memorizing code.
