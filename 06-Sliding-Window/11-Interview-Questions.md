# 🎯 Interview Questions

---

# Beginner

### 1. What is the Sliding Window Technique?

A technique that processes contiguous subarrays or substrings efficiently by maintaining a moving window.

---

### 2. When should Sliding Window be used?

Whenever the problem involves:

- Subarrays
- Substrings
- Continuous Elements
- Fixed or Variable Window Size

---

### 3. What are the two types of Sliding Window?

- Fixed Size Window
- Variable Size Window

---

### 4. Why is Sliding Window better than Brute Force?

It avoids recalculating overlapping windows, reducing Time Complexity from O(n²) to O(n).

---

### 5. What is a Fixed Size Window?

A window whose length remains constant throughout the algorithm.

---

# Intermediate

### 6. What is a Variable Size Window?

A window that expands and shrinks based on a condition.

---

### 7. Why does the window shrink?

To satisfy the problem constraints after expansion.

---

### 8. Can Sliding Window be used on Strings?

Yes.

Examples:

- Longest Substring Without Repeating Characters
- Minimum Window Substring

---

### 9. What data structures are commonly used?

- HashMap
- HashSet
- Array
- Deque

---

### 10. What is the biggest advantage?

Linear Time Complexity.
