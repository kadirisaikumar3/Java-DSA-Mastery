# 💼 Google Interview Corner

---

# What Google Evaluates

- Mathematical Reasoning
- Optimization Skills
- Number Theory
- Time Complexity Analysis
- Edge Case Handling

---

# Common Follow-up Questions

- Can you reduce the complexity?
- Can you avoid repeated calculations?
- Why is this algorithm optimal?
- Can you derive the formula?
- What are the edge cases?

---

# Edge Cases

- Zero
- One
- Prime Numbers
- Large Integers
- Overflow
- Negative Numbers

---

# Communication Tips

While solving:

- Explain the mathematical observation.
- Justify the algorithm.
- Mention Time & Space Complexity.
- Discuss edge cases.

---

# Google Advice

Strong mathematical intuition often leads to simpler and more efficient solutions.
