# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Q1. Why is GCD important?

It helps simplify fractions, compute LCM, and solve divisibility problems efficiently.

---

## Q2. Why check primes only up to √n?

If a number has a factor larger than √n, it must also have a corresponding factor smaller than √n.

---

## Q3. Why use the Sieve instead of checking each number?

The Sieve computes all primes up to `n` much faster for multiple queries.

---

## Q4. Why is Modular Arithmetic important?

It prevents integer overflow and is widely used in competitive programming and cryptography.

---

## Q5. Why is Binary Exponentiation faster?

It reduces repeated multiplication by using repeated squaring.

---

## Q6. Which companies ask Math-based DSA questions?

Google, Amazon, Microsoft, Meta, Adobe, Atlassian, Uber, and many others.

---

## Q7. Biggest interview advice?

Understand the mathematical intuition behind formulas instead of memorizing them.
