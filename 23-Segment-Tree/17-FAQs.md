# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Q1. What is the difference between Segment Tree and Fenwick Tree?

Fenwick Tree mainly supports prefix-based operations, while Segment Tree supports a wider variety of range queries and custom merge operations.

---

## Q2. When should I use Lazy Propagation?

When there are frequent range updates along with range queries.

---

## Q3. Can Segment Trees handle maximum or minimum queries?

Yes. Only the merge operation changes.

---

## Q4. Why is the build operation O(n)?

Because every array element is processed once during construction.

---

## Q5. Can Segment Trees be implemented iteratively?

Yes. Both recursive and iterative implementations are possible.

---

## Q6. Which companies ask Segment Tree questions?

Google, Amazon, Microsoft, Meta, Adobe, Uber, Bloomberg, Atlassian, Oracle, and others.

---

## Q7. Biggest interview advice?

Understand the recursion, overlap cases, and why queries and updates run in **O(log n)**.
