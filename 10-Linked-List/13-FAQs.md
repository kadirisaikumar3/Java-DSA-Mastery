# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Q1. Why use a Linked List instead of an Array?

Linked Lists allow efficient insertions and deletions without shifting elements.

---

## Q2. Why is searching slow?

Because nodes must be traversed sequentially.

---

## Q3. What is the Fast & Slow Pointer technique?

A method where one pointer moves one step and another moves two steps.

---

## Q4. What is a Dummy Node?

A temporary node placed before the head to simplify pointer manipulation.

---

## Q5. Can a Linked List be reversed in-place?

Yes.

Using pointer reversal with O(1) extra space.

---

## Q6. Which Linked List problems are most important?

- Reverse Linked List
- Merge Two Sorted Lists
- Linked List Cycle
- Add Two Numbers
- Reverse Nodes in K Group

---

## Q7. Biggest interview advice?

Draw the Linked List and track pointer movement before coding.
