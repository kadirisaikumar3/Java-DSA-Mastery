# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Q1. Why do interviews focus on Big-O?

Because it measures scalability.

---

## Q2. Which complexity matters more?

Usually Time Complexity.

However, some systems prioritize memory efficiency.

---

## Q3. Is O(log n) always better than O(n)?

Yes, for sufficiently large inputs.

---

## Q4. Why ignore constants?

Because asymptotic analysis focuses on growth rate.

---

## Q5. Does recursion always use extra space?

Yes.

Each recursive call creates a new stack frame.

---

## Q6. What is the difference between Input Space and Auxiliary Space?

Input Space stores the input data.

Auxiliary Space is the extra memory required by the algorithm.

---

## Q7. Is O(1) always faster?

Not necessarily.

Constant factors can affect actual running time, but asymptotically O(1) scales best.

---

## Q8. What should I tell the interviewer after coding?

"My solution runs in O(...) time and O(...) auxiliary space."
